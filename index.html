About Me

My name is Adam Demaree. I am currently 26 years old and have a growing passion for coding.

I am a graduate in the 2013 class at Indiana University, with a bachelor's degree in Sports Communication-Broadcast, and a minor in Telecommunications.

Talking about sports has always been a passion of mine, which led me to three years of broadcasting sports on the radio in South Bend, IN.

After moving to Indianapolis in 2016, I spent eight months as a solutions specialist at Verizon Wireless, which led me to Eleven Fifty Academy to pursue a career change.

Here at Eleven Fifty, I have discovered my passion for programming, especially in web design, and it is because of my training here that I continue to pursue a career as a .NET developer.

As you go through this portfolio, you will quickly see what I have accomplished in such a short period of time.....Welcome to my World!
